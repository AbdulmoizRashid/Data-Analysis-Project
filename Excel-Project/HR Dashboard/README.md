## Workforce & Compensation Dashboard
# Project Overview

This project is an Excel Dashboard developed during my Data Analysis internship. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce distribution, compensation metrics, and overtime analysis across different cities and provinces. It is designed to support HR and management teams in tracking employee headcount, compensation trends, and workforce structure.

# Key Features

   # Workforce Metrics
       . Total Headcount
       
       . Average Tenure

   # Compensation Metrics
       . Total Compensation
       
       . Average Compensation
       
       . Overtime & Bonus Breakdown
   # Visual Insights
       . Pie charts for employee type (Full-Time vs Contract)
       
       . Monthly Compensation trend (2005–2015)
       
       . Headcount by city and province
       
       . Count of Overtime by employment type
 
# Insights from Dashboard

  . Majority of employees are Full-Time (75%), while Contract employees make up 25%.
  
  . Karachi has the highest employee headcount among all cities.
  
  . Compensation and headcount both show clear seasonal and yearly trends.
  
  . Full-time employees contribute more significantly to overtime compared to contract employees.

# Tools & Skills Used

  . Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers, Conditional Formatting)

  . Data Cleaning & Transformation
  
  . Dashboard Design & Visualization

# Purpose

The main objective of this dashboard is to simplify workforce and compensation analysis for decision makers, enabling them to track performance, manage costs, and optimize workforce distribution effectively.
